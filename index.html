Useful by design

Apps and games made for real everyday needs

JRP Apps builds lightweight Android experiences that are easy to understand and pleasant to use. Our portfolio covers everyday calculation, personal expense tracking, fitness progress, Unicode text styling, logic puzzles and strategy games.

Productivity and utility apps

Calculator Pro supports quick calculations, while Style Text Pro turns regular words into stylish Unicode fonts and symbols for messaging and social profiles.

Finance and fitness tools

Money Mate helps users understand daily spending, and Fit Mate supports routines, progress tracking and healthier habits.

Brain games and puzzles

Arrow Escape, Water Sort Master, Sudoku and ChessMaster provide maze, sorting, number and board-game challenges for different play styles.
