Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Calculator Pro ("the App") is developed by JRP Apps.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information and how third-party services used by the App may process certain data.

Information Collection

Calculator Pro does not require you to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, phone number, contacts, or precise location.

Calculations, history, preferences, and other app data are generally stored locally on your device. If you choose to use an optional cloud-based feature, information required to provide that feature may be processed by the relevant service provider.

Permissions

The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, or photos for its core calculator functionality.

The App may use internet access and advertising-related permissions required to load advertisements, manage consent, and support optional online services.

Internet Connection

The App's core calculator functionality works without an internet connection.

An internet connection may be required for:

  • Loading advertisements
  • Managing privacy and advertising consent
  • Using optional cloud-based features
  • Accessing external support, privacy, or developer pages

Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Google AdMob to display advertisements
  • Google Firebase to support app functionality, reliability, authentication, cloud features, or diagnostics where enabled
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage advertising consent where required

These services may automatically process information such as device identifiers, IP address, advertising identifiers, app interactions, diagnostic data, crash information, and approximate location inferred from network information, depending on your device settings, consent choices, region, and the features used.

Data processed by these third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

Because the App may be used by children, advertising requests for children or users whose age is unknown are handled using child-appropriate treatment and applicable Google Play Families requirements. Such users are served only age-appropriate ads and are not served personalized advertising based on their activity.

For users who are known to be adults, advertisements may be personalized or non-personalized depending on region, consent choices, device settings, and applicable law. Where required, the App presents a consent message before requesting or using certain advertising data.

Data Sharing

JRP Apps does not sell your personal information. Limited data may be shared with or processed by third-party service providers only as necessary to provide advertising, consent management, reliability, authentication, cloud, or related app functionality.

Data Retention and Control

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you delete it through the App, clear the App's storage, or uninstall the App.

Controls relating to advertising personalization, consent, and device identifiers may also be available through the App, your device settings, or your Google account settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience, including children aged 4 and above.

JRP Apps does not knowingly request or directly collect personal information such as a child's name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, camera recordings, or microphone recordings.

When the App is used by children or by users whose age is unknown, advertising and related third-party services are configured to apply child-appropriate data treatment and applicable Google Play Families requirements. Personalized advertising is not intended to be served to children.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the App, they may contact us using the details below so that we can review the matter and take appropriate action.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the App, third-party services, legal requirements, or data practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
