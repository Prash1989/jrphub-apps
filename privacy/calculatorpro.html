Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

Calculator Pro ("the App") is developed by JRP Apps.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information and how third-party services used by the App may process certain data.

Information Collection

Calculator Pro does not require you to create an account to use its core calculator features. JRP Apps does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, camera recordings, or precise location through the App's core functionality.

Calculations, calculation history, preferences, settings, and most other app data are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to use an optional cloud-based feature, such as Cloud Backup, you may be asked to sign in with your Google account. Information required to provide authentication, synchronization, backup, restoration, or related cloud functionality may be processed by the relevant Google services.

Google Sign-In and Optional Cloud Backup

Calculator Pro may offer optional Google Sign-In and Cloud Backup functionality. These features are not required to use the App's core calculator functionality.

If you choose to sign in, Google and Firebase services may process information associated with authentication, such as your Google account identifier, authentication credentials or tokens, and information necessary to connect your account with the optional cloud feature.

App information selected or required for cloud backup may be transmitted to and stored using the relevant cloud service. Cloud data is governed by the applicable service provider's privacy policy, security practices, and retention terms.

Permissions

The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, or photos for its core calculator functionality.

The App may use internet access, network-state access, advertising identifiers, notification-related access where applicable, and other permissions required to:

  • Load and display advertisements
  • Manage privacy and advertising consent
  • Use optional online or cloud-based features
  • Retrieve exchange-rate information
  • Provide support and diagnostic functionality

The exact permissions available may vary depending on the App version, Android version, device capabilities, region, and enabled features.

Internet Connection

The App's core calculator functionality works without an internet connection.

An internet connection may be required for:

  • Loading advertisements
  • Managing privacy and advertising consent
  • Using optional Google Sign-In and Cloud Backup
  • Retrieving current exchange-rate information for the optional Currency Converter
  • Submitting an optional support report or diagnostic information
  • Accessing external support, privacy, developer, or Google service pages

Currency Converter

The App may include an optional Currency Converter that connects to an external exchange-rate service to retrieve current or recently available currency information.

When this feature is used, the external service may receive technical request information such as your IP address, request time, selected currencies, device or browser information, and network-related information necessary to process the request.

Exchange rates are provided for informational purposes and may be delayed, estimated, unavailable, or different from rates offered by banks, payment providers, exchanges, or financial institutions.

Support Reports and Diagnostics

The App may allow you to voluntarily submit a support report. A support report may include the description you provide, an optional email address if you choose to enter one, and technical information used to investigate the issue.

Technical information may include:

  • App version and build information
  • Device model and operating-system information
  • Feature status and app configuration
  • Network availability information
  • Diagnostic messages, error details, or recent app logs
  • Advertising or service-readiness information

Support information is used to investigate reported problems, improve reliability, communicate with you when contact information is provided, and protect the App from misuse.

Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Google AdMob to load, display, measure, and manage advertisements
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage advertising consent where required
  • Google Sign-In to provide optional account authentication
  • Google Firebase services to support optional authentication, cloud functionality, diagnostics, support reporting, reliability, or related app features where enabled
  • Exchange-rate service providers to provide information used by the optional Currency Converter
  • Google Play services to support Android platform functionality, app distribution, security, and service integration

Depending on the feature used, your region, your consent choices, device settings, and applicable law, these third-party services may automatically process information such as:

  • IP address
  • Device and app identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location inferred from network information
  • App interactions and advertising interactions
  • Diagnostic, performance, and crash information
  • Device model, operating system, language, and region
  • Authentication and account-related identifiers

Data processed by third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies, terms, retention rules, and security practices.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

AdMob and its advertising partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP addresses, approximate location, advertising interactions, app interactions, diagnostic information, and related technical data to load, limit, secure, measure, and report advertisements.

Advertisements may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region, age treatment, consent choices, Google account settings, device settings, and applicable law.

Where required, the App presents a consent message before certain advertising information is processed. You may be able to review or change relevant consent choices through the App, your device settings, or your Google account settings.

Children's Advertising Treatment

Because the App may be used by children, advertising requests for children or users whose age is unknown are configured to apply child-appropriate treatment and applicable Google Play Families requirements.

Such users are not intended to receive personalized advertising based on their activity. Advertisements shown to children are intended to be age appropriate and subject to the controls and limitations provided by the advertising service.

Data Sharing

JRP Apps does not sell your personal information.

Limited information may be processed by or shared with trusted third-party service providers only when reasonably necessary to provide:

  • Advertising and advertising measurement
  • Privacy and consent management
  • Authentication and optional cloud backup
  • Currency exchange-rate information
  • Support-report processing
  • Diagnostics, security, and reliability
  • Other requested online app functionality

Information may also be disclosed where required by law, legal process, regulation, or a valid request from a competent authority, or where reasonably necessary to protect users, JRP Apps, the App, or the public from fraud, abuse, security threats, or unlawful activity.

Data Retention and Control

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you delete it through the App, clear the App's storage, reset the relevant feature, or uninstall the App.

Data stored using optional Cloud Backup may remain associated with the relevant Google account or cloud service until it is deleted, replaced, expired, or otherwise handled according to the applicable service provider's retention practices.

Support-report information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to investigate the report, provide support, maintain security, resolve disputes, improve the App, and meet applicable legal obligations.

Controls relating to advertising personalization, consent, identifiers, permissions, and Google account information may also be available through the App, your device settings, your Google account settings, or the relevant service provider.

Account and Cloud Data Requests

If an optional cloud feature allows you to remove cloud information directly through the App, you may use that feature to manage the relevant data.

You may also contact JRP Apps using the email address below if you need assistance regarding information submitted directly to JRP Apps. Some authentication, advertising, platform, or cloud information is controlled independently by Google or another service provider and may need to be managed through that provider.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and may be used by children aged 4 and above.

JRP Apps does not knowingly request or directly collect a child's name, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, camera recordings, or microphone recordings through the App's core calculator functionality.

Optional features that involve account authentication, cloud services, support reports, online services, or third-party processing should be used by children with the involvement and supervision of a parent or guardian where appropriate.

When the App is used by children or users whose age is unknown, advertising and related third-party services are configured to apply child-appropriate treatment and applicable Google Play Families requirements. Personalized advertising is not intended to be served to children.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information directly to JRP Apps, they may contact us using the details below so that we can review the matter and take appropriate action.

External Links

The App may open external websites or services, including support pages, privacy pages, developer pages, Google services, advertising pages, or other third-party content.

JRP Apps does not control the privacy practices, content, security, or availability of third-party websites and services. Their use is governed by their respective policies and terms.

Security

JRP Apps takes reasonable administrative and technical steps to protect information handled directly by the App. Third-party providers may also apply their own security measures to information they process.

However, no method of electronic storage, software processing, or internet transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

International Processing

Third-party providers used by the App may process information on servers located outside your country or region. Where this occurs, information is handled according to the provider's applicable privacy policy, contractual safeguards, and legal obligations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the App, third-party services, legal requirements, security practices, or data-handling practices.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. You should review this page periodically for changes.
