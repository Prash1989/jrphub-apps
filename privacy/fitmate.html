Fit Mate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Fit Mate is developed by JRP Apps.

Information Collection

Fit Mate is designed to help users manage their personal finances. The app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal financial information.

Financial Data

Your income, expenses, budgets, categories, and reports are stored locally on your device unless you choose to use a future backup or synchronization feature.

Permissions

The app only requests permissions that are necessary for its features. No unnecessary permissions are requested.

Third-Party Services

If future versions include services such as Google AdMob, Google Drive backup, Firebase, or analytics, those services may process limited information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We do not access your financial information. Your data remains under your control on your device.

Children's Privacy

Fit Mate is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when new features or services are introduced.
