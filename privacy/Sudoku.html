Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Sudoku ("the App") is developed by JRP Apps.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Handle

The App may process gameplay progress, puzzle state, settings, achievements and basic diagnostic information needed to provide and improve the game. An account is not required for core gameplay unless a future feature explicitly states otherwise.

Local App Data

Puzzle progress, preferences, statistics and saved games may be stored locally on the device.

Permissions

The App may use internet access for advertising, consent management, updates and optional online features. It does not require access to contacts, precise location, camera or microphone for core gameplay.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App may use Google services such as Google Play Services, Google Mobile Ads and consent-management tools. These services may process device identifiers, advertising data, diagnostics and interaction information according to their own privacy policies. Where required, the App requests consent before serving personalized advertising.

Data Sharing

JRP Apps does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by the third-party services described above only to provide app functionality, advertising, security, diagnostics or legal compliance.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents or guardians may contact us with any privacy concern.

Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored app data can be removed by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it. Data processed independently by third-party services is governed by those providers' retention and deletion practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated when features, permissions or data practices change. The latest version will remain available on this page.
